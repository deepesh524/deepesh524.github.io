Hand Recognition System

Minor Project - 2018
It was one of my biggest project that made me introduced to term that is 'Artificial Intelligence'.

The project deals with the capturing your hand gestures to detect the alphabet you are trying to say.

At start, it looked like it was easy to develop such an application. But when you start to train the system about each alphabet, it gets too much difficult. I trained system about only 5 letters.

This project was a crucial one to make me interested in Artificial Intelligence.

Oriental Insurance

Internship - 2018
I was an intern an Oriental Insurance Company.

My work at Oriental Insurance Company made me realise that nowaday insurance companies are also using data to take any decisions.Before this past experiences or what they see first were used.

Big Data is an important part for an insurance company.. Analyzing data offers a deeper understanding of customer behaviour. Even insurance companies can change their premiums according to the data.

Alongwith understanding the customer behaviour, it can be used to track the progress of the company also.
